## FAQ

**Q: What robots are supported?**
A: NanoDiffBot, Arduino Nano/Uno/Mega, mBot, and mBot2.

**Q: Do I need to install software?**
A: Just Docker for the server, and the OpenRoberta Connector for USB communication.

**Q: Is it free?**
A: Yes! EduBots is completely free and open source.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: Yes, once Docker is set up, you can program robots offline.

**Q: What age group is this for?**
A: Suitable for ages 8+ with appropriate guidance.
